Welcome to Sacred Heart Convent Higher Secondary School !.

Lord Jesus
       Sacred Heart Convent Higher Secondary School was started in 1958 by Congregation of Carmelite Religious as the first  English Medium School of Mathura  District.  Today, it is a full  fledged institution which runs classes from Nursery to Std. XII, and is affiliated to the Council for the Indian Certificate of Secondary Examinations, New Delhi with code UP069. It has always enjoyed a good reputation. It has excelled in various fields and surpassed all previous records in trying to attain its aims and objectives. (Read School's History)
